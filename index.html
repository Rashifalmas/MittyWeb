About Mitty

Mitty is a 2-year-old Tubby cat with an adorable long tail and fluffy fur. She likes to mess around the house, sleep when she's tired, run away when she wants to be cleaned, doesn't like dry food, and has a strange habit of getting into cardboard boxes, no matter how small!


In 2022 we found her when she was a puppy, she was looking for food on the street by herself and we decided to adopt her and she has been the queen of our home ever since.


Now Mitty has 3 cubs named Boba, Bobi and Bubu. Mitty loves her cubs very much and so do we.
